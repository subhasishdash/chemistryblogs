Hydrochloric acid is a colorless inorganic chemical system. It has a chemical formula H2O:HCL. It has a characteristic pungent smell and classified as strong acidic in nature.


It is based on containing water which consist of hydrogen chloride and water and other varity species like hydronium and chlorine ions. It is a important chemical reagent and industrial chemical and used in production of PVC(polyvinyl chloride) for plastic. In households the dilluted hydrochloric acid is used as descaling agent.
