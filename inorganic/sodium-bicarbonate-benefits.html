What is Sodium bicarbonate?


Sodium bicarbonate, also called sodium hydrogen carbonate, or bicarbonate of soda. It is commonly known as baking soda is a chemical compound with the formula NaHCO3. It is widely used as baking also called cooking soda or bread soda. Sodium bicarbonate is a white crystalline solid, odorless, fine powder and basic properties. It is slightly salty and alkalinity in taste. It dissolves in water, so it is water-soluble, and it is non-flammable. Sodium bicarbonate is a salt is a composition of sodium ions(Na+) and bicarbonate ions(HCO3-).


Preparation of Sodium bicarbonate


NH3 + H2O + CO2 ----> NH4HCO3
NH4HCO3 + NaCl ------> NaHCO3 + NH4Cl

Benefits for Sodium bicarbonate :

  1. Natural Deodorant or Air freshner

    2. Sodium bicarbonate can be used as deodorant or as air freshner which is more natural. It is safe and much cheaper instead of using expensive brand of deodorant or air freshner. Sodium bicarbonate or baking soda is an excellent and safe alternative to commercial air fresheners. It interacts with odor particles and neutralizes them, rather than masking them.

Don’t think more is better when it comes to taking baking soda. It’s important to note that consuming too much baking soda can cause an increase in acid production.

    5. We can used baking soda or sodium bicarbonate as to clean fruits and vegetables. By soaking fruits and vegetables in a baking soda wash is the most effective way to remove pesticides without peeling them. One study found that soaking fruit in a solution of baking soda and water for 12–15 minutes removed nearly all of the pesticides.

  5. As Bathroom Cleaner
